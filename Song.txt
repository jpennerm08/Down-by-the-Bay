# Down-by-the-Bay

Down by the bay
Where the watermelons grow
Back to my home
I dare not go
For if I do
My mother will say
"You must stay
Very far away
Down at the bay"

"Did you ever see a goose
Kissing a moose?"
"Did you ever see a deer
Learning to steer"
Down by the bay.

Did you ever see a snake
Baking a cake?

Did you ever see a mouse
Painting his house?

Down by the bay.
